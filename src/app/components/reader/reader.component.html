{{title}}

Mary Lennox, a spoiled and neglected girl, is orphaned in India and sent to live with her uncle in a gloomy mansion on the Yorkshire moors. Initially, she is as sour and unpleasant as the weather, but her curiosity is piqued by a locked garden and the cries of a hidden boy. With the help of a robin, a gardener's boy, and a local girl, Mary discovers the garden and the boy, her cousin Colin. Together, they nurture the garden back to life, and in doing so, they themselves blossom, learning about friendship, resilience, and the magic of nature. [Highlighted text]
